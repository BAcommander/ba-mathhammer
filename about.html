About BA Mathhammer

BA Mathhammer is a free damage calculator that helps you determine the expected outcomes of combat in tabletop wargaming in the Far Far Future.

Features

Damage Calculator Full hit, wound, save, and damage pipeline with detailed phase breakdowns
Probability Engine Monte Carlo simulation across 1,000 battles for kill probability distributions
Multiple Weapon Profiles Model mixed units with different weapons calculated together
Battlegroup Abilities Apply offensive and defensive abilities across all profiles at once
Preset System Save and load attacker and defender profiles for quick matchup testing
Attached Characters Model character wound allocation with separate defensive profiles
Clipboard Export Copy results as images for sharing in chats, slides, and social media
Calculation Log History of calculations with save and restore for easy comparison
